About Us

Paul O'Donoghue Heating is a trusted name in Kerry for professional plumbing and heating services. Led by owner Paul O'Donoghue, we've built our reputation on delivering exceptional workmanship, outstanding customer service, and a commitment to quality. Our work is known for being clean, efficient, and professional.

We specialize in boiler installation and repair, gas heating systems, and complete heating solutions. Based in Lissivane East, Miltown, Killarney (V93 KX62), we serve Co. Kerry and surrounding areas. Paul personally ensures all customers receive the support they need. We're committed to professional service and delivering reliable heating solutions that make a real difference to your home comfort.
